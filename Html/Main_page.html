What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the gradual warming of the Earth due to changes in the climate. The rise is related to the rise of greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide. Greenhouse gases. the Earth has an atmosphere which gases can bounce off of which causes them to stay within which causes them to grow in number and consequently cause the Earth's general temperature to rise.

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances to the environment. There are multiple types of pollution but the three main types are air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution.
