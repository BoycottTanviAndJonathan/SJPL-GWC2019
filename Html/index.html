What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the gradual warming of the Earth due to changes in the climate. The rise is related to the rise of greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide.

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances to the environment. There are multiple types of pollution but the three main types are air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution.

What are Endangered Animals?

Endangered animals refers to animals who are threatened with extinction. Today in the world, there are many animals borderlining extinction due to global warming and pollution.
